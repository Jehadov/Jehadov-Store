RETURN & EXCHANGE POLICY


We understand that sometimes you don’t want an item you purchased -
so we offer you a grace period of 15 days to return your item.

To be eligible for a return, your item must be in perfect condition, the box
must be sealed, and all accessories must be included. To complete your
return, we require the original receipt.

There are certain items which are not eligible for return or refund, these
include: Gift cards.

Refunds

Once your return is received and inspected, we will send you an email to
notify you that we have received your returned item. We will also notify
you of the approval or rejection of your refund.

If you are approved, then your refund will be processed, and a credit will
automatically be applied to your credit card or original method of payment,
within 6 weeks. Refunds will be subject to the deduction of the applicable
commission paid (2% on Visa and MasterCard transactions and 0.100JD on JNet
transactions).

Late or missing refunds

If you haven’t received a refund after 6 weeks, please contact
us at Jehadmtaha@outlock.com or call us on +962 790730270.

Sale items


Only regular priced items may be refunded, unfortunately, sale items
cannot be refunded.

Exchanges

We only replace items if they are defective or damaged. If you need to
exchange it for the same item, send us an email at Jehadmtaha@outlock.com and a
customer service representative will be coordinating the exchange.

Contact Us


To return your item, simply visit the store where you purchased it or
contact us via email at Jehadmtaha@outlock.com or call us on +962 790730270
